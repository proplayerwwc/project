3/24/2020

Y5S1.1 Patch Notes

The Y5S1.1 Patch will deploy to both PC and Console on the week of March 23rd.

OBS

OBS compatibility with Vulkan (such as capture-mode) will work with the latest version of OBS Studio (v25.0).
